Yes. Every project includes at least 2 weeks of post-launch support. Retainer plans include ongoing monitoring, optimization, and updates as your business evolves.

Absolutely. We work with clients globally — USA, Europe, CIS, Middle East, and Asia. We communicate in English and Russian, and our systems support any language.
