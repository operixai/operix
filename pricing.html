Questions about pricing

Clear answers.

Are there any hidden fees?
Never. The price you see is the price you pay for setup. The only ongoing cost is your chosen retainer plan (optional) and any third-party API costs (e.g. AI model usage), which we'll estimate upfront.
Do you require payment upfront?
We typically take 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For Enterprise clients, we can structure payment milestones. We accept bank transfer, Wise, and crypto.
What if I need something between plans?
All plans are customizable. Book a free audit call and we'll scope your exact needs and give you a custom quote — no pressure, no obligation.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
If we fail to deliver what was agreed in the project scope, you get a full refund. We stand behind our work completely.
